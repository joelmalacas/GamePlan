GAMEPLAN DATABASE - MAIN RELATIONSHIPS
====================================

CORE ENTITIES:
=============
🧑 users (authentication)
🏟️ clubs (sports organizations)
👥 club_members (users in clubs)
🎯 club_roles (positions/roles)

USER & CLUB RELATIONSHIPS:
=========================
users ←→ club_members ←→ clubs
       ↑
   club_roles

users (1) ←→ (M) club_members (M) ←→ (1) clubs
club_members (M) ←→ (1) club_roles

TRAINING SYSTEM:
===============
clubs (1) ←→ (M) training_sessions
training_sessions (M) ←→ (M) club_members [via training_attendance]

training_sessions ←→ training_attendance ←→ club_members

MATCH SYSTEM:
============
clubs (1) ←→ (M) matches [as home_club]
clubs (1) ←→ (M) matches [as away_club]
matches (M) ←→ (1) competitions
matches (M) ←→ (M) club_members [via match_squads]

matches ←→ match_squads ←→ club_members
matches ←→ match_events ←→ club_members

FINANCIAL SYSTEM:
================
clubs (1) ←→ (M) financial_transactions
financial_categories (1) ←→ (M) financial_transactions
club_members (1) ←→ (M) member_payments
clubs (1) ←→ (M) member_payments

STATISTICS:
==========
club_members (1) ←→ (M) player_statistics [by season]
clubs (1) ←→ (M) club_statistics [by season/competition]

COMMUNICATIONS:
==============
clubs (1) ←→ (M) announcements
users (1) ←→ (M) notifications
clubs (1) ←→ (M) notifications

MEDICAL:
=======
club_members (1) ←→ (M) medical_records

MAIN FOREIGN KEYS:
=================
club_members.user_id → users.id
club_members.club_id → clubs.id
club_members.role_id → club_roles.id
training_sessions.club_id → clubs.id
training_attendance.training_session_id → training_sessions.id
training_attendance.member_id → club_members.id
matches.home_club_id → clubs.id
matches.away_club_id → clubs.id
matches.competition_id → competitions.id
match_squads.match_id → matches.id
match_squads.member_id → club_members.id
financial_transactions.club_id → clubs.id
financial_transactions.category_id → financial_categories.id
member_payments.club_id → clubs.id
member_payments.member_id → club_members.id
player_statistics.member_id → club_members.id
club_statistics.club_id → clubs.id
medical_records.member_id → club_members.id
notifications.user_id → users.id
announcements.club_id → clubs.id

TABLE CATEGORIES BY COLOR:
=========================
🟢 USERS & AUTH: users, user_sessions
🔵 CLUBS: clubs, club_roles, club_members, club_invitations
🟠 TRAINING: training_sessions, training_attendance, training_exercises
🟡 MATCHES: competitions, matches, match_squads, match_events
🟣 FINANCE: financial_categories, financial_transactions, member_payments
🔴 COMMUNICATION: announcements, notifications
📊 STATS: player_statistics, club_statistics
🏥 MEDICAL: medical_records
